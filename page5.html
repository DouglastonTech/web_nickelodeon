Jade Pettyjohn was born on November 8, 2000. Jade is a 16 year old actress who is mostly known as her role Summer in School of Rock and Mckenna Brooks in An American Girl Movie: Mckenna Shoots for the Stars. She was in the films Dakota's Summer, Girl Flu as Bird, S.H.M.I.L.U. as Bella. She was also in other TV shows and films. She was even in one video game. Lastly, she played the role of Cat in Rufus 2.
