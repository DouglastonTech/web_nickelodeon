JACE NORMAN
Jace Norman started off as a young actor. He was awarded not too long ago as the best young male actor. Jace has two siblings, Xander Norman and Glory Norman. He was born March 21st, 2001. At the age of 13, he guest starred on the hit show The Thundermans, who were generously gifted a Nickelodeon Kids Choice Award Blimp. Then he continued onto another hit show on Nickelodeon titled  Henry Danger. Then he starred in the movie Splitting Adam, as well as Spark and Rufus which will have a Rufus 2  in January 2017.
