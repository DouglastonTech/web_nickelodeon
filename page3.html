Kira Kosarin was born on October 7th 1997.  Her parents were Broadway actors. Her mother was an actress and her father had multiple roles as  a music.director, conductor and record producer. Kira was on The Thundermans, as the lead role. She was on two episodes of Paradise Run, One Crazy Cruise as Ellie Bauer. She was a guest star on School of Rock as Princess Oliviana, Danger and Thunder as Phoebe Thunderman, and Shake it Up as Rania Kumar. She was herself in the Ho Ho Holiday Special and Awesomeness TV. Finally, she was on the Nickelodeon Not-So Valentines Day Special.
